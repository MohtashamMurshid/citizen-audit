# CitizenAudit Agent

Crowdsourced + AI-powered field verification of ISI products

How it works:

Citizen uploads photo of a product from a local shop
OCR Agent extracts ISI number
Verification Agent checks BIS database
Geolocation Agent maps fake product hotspots across India
Builds a live heatmap of counterfeit product concentration
Escalation Agent batches reports and sends to BIS regional offices

Why it's novel: Crowdsourced intelligence + agentic automation + real policy impact

Overall Objectives
- Promote awareness and understanding of open standards among students.
- Encourage innovation through open technologies and standards-based solutions.
- Bridge the gap between academia and national standardization bodies.
- Provide hands-on exposure to the standards development process.
- Inspire students to actively contribute to India’s evolving standards ecosystem.

Through this collaborative initiative, the event seeks to empower students not only as developers and innovators but also as contributors to the nation’s quality and compliance framework.
